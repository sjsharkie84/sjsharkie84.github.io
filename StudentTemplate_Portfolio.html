Artist Statement

At a very young age I had developed a strong interest in video games. The first computer I ever owned was a "DX4", which was before Intel developed the Pentium computers. The first video game that I owned was called "Rampage: World Tour." Ever since then I would build my own computers by studying the hardware as well as software involved. I knew which video cards as well as hard drives were the latest.

My passion for video games is what made me want to develop my artistic skills in the first place. There was and is still a strong belief that in order to learn how to create characters as well as develop a story in a video game involves being familiar with developmental software, such as Photoshop and Illustrator. "Winning isn't Everything. It is the only thing", which was a quote by Vince Lombardi, who was the Former Coach of the Green Bay Packers. That is something I carry to this day as my inspiration.

In deciding what theme I wanted to portray was a no-brainer. I wanted to explain in detail a passion of mine that I have had for several years, which was and is still playing video games. Some of the best games that I have played included Witcher 2, Batman: Arkham Origins and Elder Scrolls: Skyrim. My goal is to highlight some of the good aspects of these games. These games came out fairly recent and are considered classics in terms of graphics and gameplay."
